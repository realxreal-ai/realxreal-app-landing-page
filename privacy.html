Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At realxreal, privacy isn't a feature—it's our foundation. We built this app on the principle that your identity, your relationships, and your memories belong to you alone. This policy explains exactly what data we collect (almost nothing), why we collect it (to make the app work), and how we protect it (cryptographically).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information never leaves your device:

  • Contact Information: Names and identifiers for your trusted contacts
  • Memory Challenges: Questions and answers you create for verification
  • Cryptographic Keys: Private keys stored in your device's Secure Enclave
  • Authentication History: Local logs of verification attempts
  • Biometric Data: Face ID/Touch ID data (never transmitted, processed entirely by iOS)

1.2 Information We Process

To enable the app's core functionality, we process minimal anonymous data:

  • Firebase User ID: A random identifier that allows notifications and contact discovery (not linked to your real identity)
  • Push Notification Tokens: Required to deliver verification requests
  • Verification Status: Anonymous signals that authentication occurred (no content of challenges or answers)

1.3 Information We Explicitly Do NOT Collect

  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses (unless you voluntarily contact support)
  • Location data
  • Contacts from your address book
  • Conversation content
  • Browsing or usage patterns beyond basic analytics
  • Any personally identifiable information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Enable cryptographic authentication between trusted contacts
  • Deliver push notifications when verification is requested
  • Maintain encrypted authentication history on your device

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Anonymous crash reports to fix bugs (opt-in only)
  • Aggregated, anonymous usage statistics to improve features

2.3 What We Never Do

  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Train AI models on your data
  • Create profiles or track behavior across apps

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All sensitive data is stored locally on your device using:

  • iOS Keychain: For cryptographic keys
  • Secure Enclave: For biometric authentication
  • Encrypted SQLite: For contact and memory data

3.2 Cloud Infrastructure

Our minimal cloud services (Firebase) are configured with:

  • Anonymous user identifiers only
  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Encryption at rest
  • No permanent storage of verification content
  • Automatic deletion of notification tokens when unused

3.3 Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We cannot decrypt your data because we never have the keys. Even if our servers were compromised, an attacker would find nothing of value because all authentication happens on-device.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Service Providers

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Push notifications and anonymous authentication
  • Apple Push Notification Service: Notification delivery

These providers have their own privacy policies and security practices. We've configured them to collect minimal data.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, but given our architecture, we have almost nothing to disclose. We cannot provide:

  • Memory challenge content (stored only on devices)
  • Decrypted user data (we don't have keys)
  • Verification history (stored only on devices)

5. Your Rights and Controls

5.1 Data Access

You have complete access to all data stored on your device through the app interface.

5.2 Data Deletion

  • Delete individual contacts: Remove specific trusted contacts and their associated data
  • Delete memory challenges: Remove verification questions at any time
  • Delete your account: Delete your Firebase UID and all associated cloud data (Settings → Delete Account)
  • Uninstall the app: Removes all local data immediately

5.3 Data Portability

Your data lives on your device. You can back it up through iOS device backups (encrypted).

5.4 Opt-Out Rights

  • Crash reporting: Settings → Privacy → Crash Reports
  • Analytics: Settings → Privacy → Analytics
  • Push notifications: iOS Settings → realxreal → Notifications

6. Children's Privacy

realxreal is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will delete it immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Since data is stored primarily on your device, there are no international transfers. The minimal cloud data (Firebase UIDs and notification tokens) may be processed on Google's global infrastructure but contains no personally identifiable information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Updates on this page (with revised "Last Updated" date)

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your data (available in-app)
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of data "sales" (we never sell data)

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

European users have rights under GDPR:

  • Right of Access: Access your data (stored on your device)
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing

To exercise these rights, email privacy@realxreal.ai
