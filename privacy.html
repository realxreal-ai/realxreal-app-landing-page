Our Commitment to Privacy

At realxreal, privacy isn't a feature—it's our foundation. We built this app on the principle that your identity, your relationships, and your memories belong to you alone.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information never leaves your device:

  • Contact Information: Names and identifiers for your trusted contacts
  • Memory Challenges: Questions and answers you create for verification
  • Cryptographic Keys: Private keys stored in your device's Secure Enclave
  • Authentication History: Local logs of verification attempts
  • Biometric Data: Face ID/Touch ID data (never transmitted)

1.2 Information We Process

To enable the app's core functionality, we process minimal anonymous data:

  • Firebase User ID: A random identifier (not linked to your real identity)
  • Push Notification Tokens: Required to deliver verification requests

1.3 Information We Explicitly Do NOT Collect

  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Location data
  • Contacts from your address book
  • Conversation content

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Enable cryptographic authentication between trusted contacts
  • Deliver push notifications when verification is requested

2.3 What We Never Do

  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Train AI models on your data

3. Data Storage and Security

All sensitive data is stored locally using iOS Keychain, Secure Enclave, and Encrypted SQLite. Our minimal cloud services (Firebase) utilize TLS 1.3 encryption and Zero-Knowledge Architecture.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We use Firebase (Google) and Apple Push Notification Service for delivery. These providers collect minimal data required for transmission.

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete access to all data stored on your device. You can delete contacts, challenges, or your entire account via Settings at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

realxreal is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt-out. Since we collect almost no data, these requests are easily handled via the app or email.

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

European users have rights to access, rectification, erasure, and restriction. Contact us at privacy@realxreal.ai.
